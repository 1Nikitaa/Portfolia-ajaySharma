About US

Way of life Yog Sadhna Kendra, as a part of BANSWARA KARATE SANSTHAN is a premier institution to learn about the most authentic yog ,as taught by Patanjali Ashtanga yog padhti.Yog word has been derived from yuj dhatu meaning -“TO JOIN”.

evam parampara praptam imam rajarsayo viduh sa kaleneha mahata yogo nastah parantapa sa evayam maya te dya yogah proktah puratanah bhakto si me sakha ceti rahasyam hy etad uttamam

(Bhagavad Gita 4.1-3)

TRANSLATION: Krishna said to his disciple Arjun: I instructed this ancient science of yoga to the sun-god, Vivasvan, and Vivasvan instructed it to Manu, and Manu in turn instructed it to Ikṣvaku. Saints of highest orders learnt it thus in disciplic succession . But in due time, this supreme science got lost. That very ancient science of the yog is today told by Me to you because you are my friend
